Privacy Policy

How APOLLO CyberSentinel protects your privacy and data

Our Commitment to Privacy

At APOLLO CyberSentinel, protecting your privacy is fundamental to our mission. As a cybersecurity company dedicated to defending against nation-state threats, we understand the critical importance of data privacy and security.

Information We Collect

Automatic Data Collection

APOLLO CyberSentinel operates with zero telemetry by design. We do not collect:

  • Personal browsing data
  • File contents or names
  • Usage analytics or telemetry
  • Location data
  • Communication content

Threat Intelligence Data

For protection purposes, APOLLO may process:

  • Threat signatures - Hashes and patterns of known threats (anonymized)
  • Network indicators - Suspicious IP addresses and domains (no personal data)
  • System health data - Operating system version and security status (local only)

Support Communications

When you contact us for support, we may collect:

  • Email address and contact information you provide
  • Support ticket content and communications
  • System logs relevant to technical issues (only with your consent)

How We Use Information

Local Processing

All threat analysis and protection occurs locally on your device. APOLLO does not send your data to external servers for analysis.

Threat Intelligence Updates

We may update threat signatures and protection rules, but these updates:

  • Contain no personal data
  • Are delivered securely and encrypted
  • Do not track individual installations

Data Sharing and Disclosure

Third-Party Services

APOLLO integrates with threat intelligence services for enhanced protection:

  • VirusTotal - File hash checking (no file contents shared)
  • Shodan - Network threat intelligence
  • AlienVault OTX - Community threat feeds

These services receive only anonymized threat indicators, never personal data.

Legal Requirements

We may disclose information only when:

  • Required by valid legal process
  • Necessary to protect against imminent harm
  • To defend our legal rights

Data Security

We employ industry-leading security measures:

  • End-to-end encryption - All communications encrypted
  • Local data storage - Your data stays on your device
  • Zero-knowledge architecture - We cannot access your protected data
  • Regular security audits - Continuous security assessment

Your Rights and Choices

Data Control

You have complete control over your data:

  • Access - Review what data is processed locally
  • Deletion - Uninstall removes all local data
  • Portability - Export your configuration settings
  • Opt-out - Disable specific protection features

Communication Preferences

You can control:

  • Security notifications and alerts
  • Product updates and announcements
  • Support communications

Children's Privacy

APOLLO CyberSentinel is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we learn we have collected such information, we will delete it immediately.

International Data Transfers

As a local-first application, APOLLO processes data primarily on your device. Any necessary data transfers are:

  • Limited to threat intelligence updates
  • Encrypted and anonymized
  • Compliant with applicable data protection laws

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. We will:

  • Notify users of material changes
  • Post updates on our website
  • Maintain previous versions for reference
